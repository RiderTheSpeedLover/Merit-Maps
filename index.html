FAQ

Good to know

Where does the cutoff data come from?

Directly from the Maharashtra State CET Cell CAP Round I–IV cutoff PDFs for A.Y. 2025-26, Mumbai University region (college codes 03xxx).

Why are placement and fees marked approximate or blank?

The official CAP data contains no placement or fee figures. Placement is a rough estimate for tier comparison only; fees are left blank for you to fill from the official Fee Regulating Authority list.

What does the cutoff number actually mean?

It's the merit percentile of the last candidate admitted to that seat — shown here as the lowest it reached across the four rounds.

Is this affiliated with the CET Cell?

No. MeritMap is an independent reference tool. Always confirm final admission decisions on the official CET Cell and college portals.
